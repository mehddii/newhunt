A Modern Tool for UVa Algorithm Problem Discovery

NewHunt is a modern version of uhunt that is designed to help users discover interesting algorithm problems to solve on UVa Online Judge. It retains the core functionalities of its predecessor, offering a streamlined and enhanced experience for finding engaging challenges and tracking your problem-solving progress on UVa.

Quick Access & Explore UVA Problems

Quick Problem Search

Instantly search the entire UVA Online Judge problem set by ID or keywords.

Browse Problem Collections

Explore a wide variety of UVA problem collections.

Access User Stats

View your submission history, rankings, and other personal statistics.

Leaderboard

See the top performers and track your progress against the community.

Community Blog

Read articles, tutorials, and announcements from the NewHunt community (like Codeforces).

Problem Solving Board

A dedicated space to jot down ideas, notes, and collaborate with others while tackling problems.

Online Editor

Write, compile, and test your code directly within the browser for a seamless experience.

Explore and Engage

Learn

Access tutorials, guides, and resources to enhance your algorithm knowledge.

Practice

Dive into a wide range of UVA problems to sharpen your coding skills.

Chat

Connect with fellow problem solvers, discuss challenges, and share insights.
